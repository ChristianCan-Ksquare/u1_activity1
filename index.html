Greatest Achievements

  • Discovering of the Hawking Radiation. Instead of understanding all black holes as simple gravitational gluttons that can only ever grow in mass, Hawking Radiation explains how black holes can actually leak energy to the point of disappearance.
  • Explanation of the Creation of the Universe. Applying the concepts of Quantum physics and general relativity he proved that the Universe was created due to the explosion of singularity trapped in an infinetly small space and that no creator was involved in the process. This theory was widely accepted and also helped scrutinize the behaviour of black holes and furthermore the nature of the universe.
  • His book, A Brief History of Time, became an international best-seller, making Sthepen one of the world's most successful popularizers of science.
  • He became one of the most famous scientist in the world despite having a severe physical disability caused by motor neurone disease.
